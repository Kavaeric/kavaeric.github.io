SHOJI USHIYAMA

I'm Shoji Ushiyama, but I mostly go by my online alias, Kavaeric. I'm a 23-year-old Japanese artist from Hong Kong. Most use he/him/his with me.

My main personal pursuit is a science-fiction worldbuilding project. I draw a lot of furries. I also take interest in drawing fashion, firearms and guns, scenery, cars, spaceships, graphic design, among other things.

Art aside, I can do some programming work, do front-end web development, and have an interest in computers in general. I also have interests in music composition and 3D modelling, but would still like much improvement those areas.

I am currently attending OCAD University in Toronto, Canada, for an undergraduate degree in Industrial Design.

I have an FAQ page that should answer most common questions. If not, feel free to contact me, and I'll do my best to answer.
